Resources

Virginia Tech provides a wide array of mental health services and resources to support your well-being.

Individual Support
  • Counselors, Psychiatric Care, 24 hour Crisis Support - Cook Counseling Center
  • TimelyCare - Virtual mental health support with counselors, a TalkNow option, health coaching, and more
  • Academic Support, Crisis Support - Dean of Students
  • Accommodations and Resources - Services for Students with Disabilities
  • Gender-based Violence Support and Advocacy - Women's Center at Virginia Tech
  • 24 Hour call line - NRVCS (Not VT affiliated)
Group Support
  • Group Therapy - Cook Counseling Center
  • Support Groups - Non-clinical, staff facilitated
  • Cultural and Community Centers
