Try box breathing

Slow breathing can signal safety to your nervous system. If it feels uncomfortable, stop and try a gentler activity instead.

2-minute practice

  1. Breathe in through your nose for 4 counts.
  2. Hold softly for 4 counts.
  3. Breathe out through your mouth for 4 counts.
  4. Hold empty for 4 counts.
  5. Repeat 4 rounds—or fewer if that’s plenty.

If you’re in immediate danger or thinking about hurting yourself, please use VT crisis resources (mock link below)—you don’t have to handle this alone.
